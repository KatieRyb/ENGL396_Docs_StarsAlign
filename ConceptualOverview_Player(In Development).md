# **Conceptual Overview \- Player (Still in Development)**

## Stars Align Video Game

# **Introduction**

This conceptual overview explains what the player should know about the game. This information would be translated into a proper format onto steam or any other game distribution platform so that the player can review before downloading.

# **Technology**

- This game is made in Ren’Py  
- Ren’Py is supported on Windows, recent versions of macOS and Linux

# **Game Genre**

- Visual novel  
- Puzzle  
- Escape room  
- Narrative  
- Mystery 

# **Gaming Experience**

A player in this game can assist the MC with her day to day tasks, manipulating the situation to their advantage to find out more about her and the elusive astrologer who seems quite comfortable giving everyone orders. Solve puzzles, communicate with the main character and convince her to confront the truth hidden in that tiny room.

As the player you can:

- Interact with the main character  
- Solve puzzles  
- Read story  
- Unlock achievements
