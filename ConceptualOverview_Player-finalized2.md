# **Gaming Experience**

A player in this game can assist the MC with her day to day tasks, manipulating the situation to their advantage to find out more about her and the elusive astrologer who seems quite comfortable giving everyone orders. Solve puzzles, communicate with the main character and convince her to confront the truth hidden in that tiny room.

As the player you can:

- Interact with the main character  
- Solve puzzles  
- Read story  
- Unlock achievements

The game follows a repetitive day loop that you as the player can modify by manipulating Phoebe's character traits, using technology provided to you by the Astrologer. The player starts off with a few characteristics defining Phoebe; however as the story progresses by you solving puzzles and making different choices, you will unlock new personality traits to add to her constellation. These changes will further alter the events that take place during the day cycle as well as Phoebe’s reactions to her surroundings.
